# Dashboard Documentation

# Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

The Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence solution developed in Tableau to analyze retail sales performance. The project is divided into three dashboards to provide stakeholders with executive, product, and customer insights.

---

# Dashboard 1: Executive Overview

## Purpose

Provides a high-level overview of the organization's sales performance.

## KPIs

- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Average Order Value
- Sales per Customer
- Total Quantity Sold
- Total Products

## Visualizations

- KPI Cards
- Sales by Category
- Monthly Sales Trend

---

# Dashboard 2: Country & Product Insights

## Purpose

Analyzes geographical sales performance and identifies top-performing products.

## Visualizations

- Sales by Country (Map)
- Top 10 Products by Sales

---

# Dashboard 3: Customer Insights

## Purpose

Identifies high-value customers based on sales performance.

## Visualizations

- Top 10 Customers by Sales

---

# Interactive Features

- Dashboard Navigation Buttons
- Interactive Filters
- Dynamic KPI Cards
- Tooltips
- Sorting
- Drill-down Analysis

# Business Insights

- Monitor overall sales performance.
- Identify top-selling products.
- Analyze sales across different countries.
- Track customer purchasing behavior.
- Observe monthly sales trends.
- Support business decision-making through interactive dashboards.
